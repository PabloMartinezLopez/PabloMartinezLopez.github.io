Big Dad Wolf

by Pablo Martínez López

Side scrolling runner.

The player must cross the level avoiding obstacles.

The character constantly move forward and must slide down and jump to get coins and avoid the obstacles to reach the goal.

Every time that the character crashes with an obstacle will lower life, and if life reaches zero will lose.

The controls are: up arrow to jump and down arrow to slide.
