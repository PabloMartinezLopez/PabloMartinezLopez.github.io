Hit Me Hard

by Pablo Martínez López as programer and game designer

Street fighting game

3D fighting game with 2D mechanics for two players and arcade single player.

It's a fighting game with a futuristic touch.

The game takes places in a city called Ark. HMH is what was considered an illegal fighting league in the Ark, but eventually was legalized after proper security measures.

The game have a combo system to hit your opponent combining punches and kicks to do special abilities and deal more damage to your opponent.

Although the game has a campaign with main story, versus mode for players with rankings is the main feature.

The player can improve the characters and unlock new sponsors and his energy drinks which potentiate certain aspects of the character.

Objective of the project

HMH started as an end-of-career project. It was in development for about a year. We try to perform complex combos mechanics and AAA visual quality to test our abilities and push our own limits. Unfortunately when the course ended the group was separated. We create around the game an attractive fiction to expand the marketing of the project. Within this fiction there are sponsors and brands of energy drinks with which we could sell merchandaising.



Current project status

Because of the separation of the group the project could not go forward. There is no playable version but you can see the source code of what was developed and the social networks of the project.
